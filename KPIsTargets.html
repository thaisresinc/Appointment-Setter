HOW IT ALL CONNECTS
Activity → Results → Income
Your KPIs aren't isolated numbers. They form a chain — each one feeds the next.
180 dials per day
Volume is non-negotiable. You can't control who picks up — but you control how many times you dial. More dials = more conversations.
~10% contact rate → conversations
Roughly 1 in 10 dials connects. At 180 dials, that's ~18 real conversations per day to work with.
Conversations → 5+ bookings per day
Not every conversation converts — but consistent conviction and qualification get you there. Quality matters as much as volume here.
Bookings → 70% confirmation rate
Good booking notes, correct qualification, and timely confirmation calls keep your sit rate strong. Unqualified bookings kill this number.
Confirmed sits → 3 sales/week → commission unlocked
Every confirmed sit is an opportunity for a specialist to close. 3 sales per week unlocks your commission threshold. That starts with your dials today.

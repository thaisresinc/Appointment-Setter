Your Schedule

Your team runs on a fortnightly rotating roster. Here's how to read it and what to expect each week.

HOW THE ROSTER WORKS
Two Weeks, Two Patterns
🔄
Early & late shifts rotate
If you're on an early shift in Week 1, you'll be on a late shift in Week 2 — and vice versa. The shifts stay the same; who's on them changes.
📅
Saturdays rotate fortnightly
You work every second Saturday and get the full weekend off the other week. Over the fortnight, everyone works one Saturday.
📞
Wednesday & Thursday are the fullest days
Most of the team is on the floor on Wed and Thu — expect a full floor, high energy, and the best call momentum of the week.
🔃
Shift questions or swaps
For anything roster-related — questions, swaps, or changes — see Jordan directly. Don't sort swaps between yourselves without TL sign-off.

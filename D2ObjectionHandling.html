THE FRAMEWORK
DTRC — Four Steps, Every Time
Every objection — regardless of what it is — gets the same four-step response. Master the framework first. Then the library fills itself in.
D
DISARM
Lower their guard before anything else. Acknowledge the objection without fighting it.
"I'm really glad you brought that up…"
T
TRANSITION
Bridge from their concern to your response. Reframe without dismissing.
"That's actually one of the most common misconceptions…"
R
RESPOND
Deliver the sub-script. This is the specific response for this specific objection.
[See the library below — each objection has its own R]
C
CHECK-IN
Move them forward with a softened close. Don't pitch — guide.
"Based on that, would you be opposed to…"
💡 The R changes per objection. The D, T, and C are mostly the same — you'll develop your own natural versions of those. The library below gives you the R for every common scenario. Always wrap it: D → T → R → C.

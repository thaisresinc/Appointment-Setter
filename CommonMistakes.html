What Great Setters Do Differently
01
Stay calm and professional
Never take objections personally. Composed, respectful, confident — always.
02
Listen actively and fully
Let the prospect speak without interruption. Understand before responding.
03
Clarify before responding
Ask follow-up questions to find the real objection — not the surface one.
04
Keep responses simple and relevant
Address the concern clearly and concisely. No information dump.
05
Transition back to the appointment smoothly
After handling the objection, guide the conversation back to booking. Don't let it drift.

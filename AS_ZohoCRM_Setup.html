PART 1 — HOME PAGE SETUP
Setting Up Your Cockpit
Your home page shows everything due today — callbacks, follow-ups, tasks. Set it up once and it works for you every single shift.
SETUP STEPS
1
Go to your Home screen
Click Home in the left sidebar — same as the screenshot on the right.
2
Click the 3 dots (⋯) — top right
You'll see options: Add Component, Reorder, View in Full Screen.
3
Click Add Component
Select Custom View → choose the Tasks module.
4
Search "My" and add all 4 components
Type my in the search box — only add the "My" versions. See the 4 components below.
5
Drag to reorder
Once added, drag the components into whatever order suits you best.
⚠️
Always choose the "My" versions — e.g. My Today's Tasks, not "Today's Tasks". The "My" version shows only YOUR tasks, not the whole team's.

THE 4 COMPONENTS TO ADD
My Today's Tasks
Everything due today — callbacks, bookings to confirm, follow-ups. This is your starting point every morning.
CHECK FIRST
My Overdue Tasks
Tasks from yesterday and earlier that haven't been completed. Red signals — these need immediate attention.
ACTION NEEDED
My Tomorrow's Tasks
Tasks set for the next day — lets you plan ahead and prepare for tomorrow's callbacks before you log off.
PLAN AHEAD
My Next 7 Days + Overdue
Full view of all leads to call including overdue ones. The complete picture of your pipeline for the week.

USE CASE — CATCHING A RESCHEDULE
1
Navigate to the relevant date — e.g. go back to yesterday to review what happened to your appointments.
2
Spot a reschedule on the list — it'll show as Rescheduled status in your breakdown.
3
Click on the appointment, then click the blue link to open the meeting record.
4
Review the notes left by the confirmation team — understand what happened with the booking.
5
If it's feasible, jump on the reschedule immediately — call the customer and get them rebooked before the opportunity slips away.
⚡
Speed matters with reschedules. The longer you wait, the colder the lead gets. Check your dashboard every morning and act on reschedules the same day you spot them.
PART 3 — TIMEZONE SETTING
Set Your Time Zone Correctly
Do this once — it affects every task, callback, and appointment in the system.
SETTINGVALUE
Time Zone(GMT+10:00) Australian Eastern Standard Time — New South Wales / Sydney
🇮🇩 Bali team: Set your Zoho timezone to GMT+10 to match Australian Eastern time — not your local Bali time (GMT+8). All appointments, tasks, and callbacks run on AU Eastern time. If your timezone is wrong, your tasks will show at the wrong times and you'll miss callbacks.

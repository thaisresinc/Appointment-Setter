THE FORMAT — MEMORISE THIS
Booking Notes Structure
Every booking note in the CRM follows this exact format. No exceptions — the confirmation team and specialist rely on this structure.
WHAT IT LOOKS LIKE IN THE CRM
ZOHO CRM — NOTE
BOOKING NOTES

WHO WILL ATTEND: Both present at 9am

GENERAL NOTES: Customer aware of battery rebate. Quarterly bill $580. Keen to reduce costs before winter.
THE 4 SECTIONS
DECISION MAKERS
Who owns the decision — both partners? Solo homeowner?
WHO WILL ATTEND
Who will actually be at the appointment
ROOF CHECK
No shading issues OR Willing to trim
GENERAL NOTES
Anything else relevant — bill amount, motivations, objections handled
ONE-TIME SETUP
The Claude Template Prompt
Copy this prompt, paste it into a new Claude chat, and save that chat. You'll reuse it for every booked call from here on.
CLAUDE PROMPT — COPY AND PASTE THIS ONCE
"Create a booking notes template. I'll upload call transcripts — read them line by line and generate booking notes in this exact format for an already-booked customer: BOOKING NOTES DECISION MAKERS: WHO WILL ATTEND: ROOF CHECK: No shading issues OR Willing to trim GENERAL NOTES: Output as plain text."
💡
Save this chat in Claude. Every time you have a booked call, come back to this same chat and paste the transcript. The template is already set up — you just add the transcript and get the notes.
THE PROCESS
5 Steps — After Every Booked Call
1
Set up your Claude template
ONE TIME ONLY
Paste the prompt above into a new Claude chat. Save the chat — you'll return to it after every booked call. You only do this once.
2
Pull the transcript from the dialer
AFTER BOOKING
  • Go to Reporting → Dials
  • Filter by Disposition: Booked and your name
  • Click Play on the call
  • Hover just below the call ID → Ctrl + C to copy the transcript
3
Paste transcript into Claude → generate notes
30 SECONDS
Open your saved Claude chat → paste the transcript → press Enter. Claude generates the booking notes instantly.

Want more detail? Type: "Add more detail and sales-relevant context."
4
Paste into Zoho CRM as plain text
CRITICAL STEP
  • Open a new note on the lead in Zoho CRM
  • Title: BOOKING NOTES
  • Body: paste using Ctrl + Shift + V — not Ctrl+V

See below for why this matters ↓
5
Tidy up and save
FINAL STEP
  • Delete any leftover AI commentary or preamble
  • Bold the BOOKING NOTES header and all 4 section titles
  • Read through to confirm accuracy
  • Click Save
WHY IT MATTERS
Always Paste with Ctrl+Shift+V
❌ CTRL + V — DON'T USE THIS
Ctrl + V
Pastes with formatting — creates messy spacing, broken line breaks, inconsistent fonts in the CRM. Looks unprofessional.
✅ CTRL + SHIFT + V — USE THIS
Ctrl + Shift + V
Pastes as plain text — clean, consistent, professional. Exactly what the specialist and confirmation team see.
💡
Mac users: the equivalent is Cmd + Shift + V — same principle.

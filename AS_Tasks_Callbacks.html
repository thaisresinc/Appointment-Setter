PART 1 — STEP BY STEP
How to Create a Task After Every Call
Do this immediately after finishing a conversation — before moving to the next lead.
1
Open the lead in CRM
After finishing the call on the dialer, navigate to the lead record in Zoho CRM.
2
Hover over Open Activities → click +
Find the Open Activities section → hover to reveal the + button → click it.
3
Select Task
From the dropdown, choose Task.
4
Write a specific Subject
Be descriptive — explain when and why to call. See examples on the right.
5
Set the Date
Set it to the correct callback day.
6
Set a Reminder — 5 min before
Click Reminder → set 5 minutes before the callback time. You'll get an email alert so nothing slips.
7
Set Owner to yourself → Save
Make sure the task is assigned to you, then click Save.
SUBJECT LINE EXAMPLES
Customer at work until 5pm
CB after 5pm — at work
Customer wants early morning call
CB before 9am — interested
Customer asked for weekend
CB Saturday — requested
Customer busy, try again tomorrow
CB tomorrow — try again
💡
Be specific in the subject line. "Callback" alone tells you nothing. "CB after 5pm — at work" tells you exactly when to call and why. Future you will thank present you.

PART 2 — COLOUR CODES
What the Colours Mean
The Next Action at the top of every lead is colour coded. Read it at a glance before every call.
🟠
Orange
Due today — call this lead before end of shift
🔴
Red
Overdue — either needs working today OR has been left as the Day 5 re-upload signal
🟢
Green
Set for a future date — scheduled and coming up

PART 3 — THE CALLBACK CYCLE
The 5-Day Callback Cycle
Every no-answer gets 5 days. Click each day to see exactly what to do.
🔴
AFTER DAY 5 — LEAVE IT RED
If the customer still hasn't picked up after 5 days, leave the task as overdue (red). Do not mark it complete.

A red overdue activity signals to management that this lead was once a quality callback opportunity — the customer showed interest. That tells them the lead can be re-uploaded to the dialer when appropriate, giving the team another chance to reach the customer.

Red doesn't mean failure. It means you've done your job for 5 days and management will handle the rest.
QUICK REFERENCE
The Callback Rules in 30 Seconds
→Create a task after every call where a callback was discussed
→Subject line: specific — when and why, not just "callback"
→Call 1–3 times per day on each callback lead
→No answer → edit task date to next day and repeat
→After Day 5: leave red — do not mark complete
→Red = re-upload signal for management — not failure

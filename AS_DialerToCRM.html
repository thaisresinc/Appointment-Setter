THE PROCESS
Step by Step — After Every Call
1
Acknowledge the callback in the dialer
After calling the lead, acknowledge the callback in the dialer. The lead should already be loaded in the CRM from the preloaded link.
2
Open the lead in CRM → hover Open Activities → click +
Find the Open Activities section → hover to reveal the + button → click it → select Task.
3
Set the task for today
For a no answer, set a callback for the current day — a few hours later. Set the owner to yourself.
4
Add a reminder — delivered via email
Set a reminder for a few hours before the callback. Delivered via email. Set to never repeat.
5
Save
Click Save. The open activity now appears in the lead and on your home page task list.
YOUR DAILY CALL RHYTHM
2–3 Attempts Per Callback Per Day
☀️ MORNING
Work callbacks first — before new leads
These are committed follow-ups. They go before anything else in the queue.
☕ MIDDAY
Second attempt on same-day callbacks
If no answer in the morning, try again mid-shift.
🌆 END OF SHIFT
Final attempt — then roll to next day
If still no answer after 2–3 attempts, edit the task date to tomorrow.
THE CYCLE
Work Every Lead for 5 Business Days
After each day of no answer — click ⋯ → Edit → change date to next business day.

To roll forward: Open Activities → 3 dots (⋯) → Edit → change date to next business day → Save. Takes 10 seconds.
🔴
AFTER DAY 5 — LEAVE IT RED
Once you've worked the lead for 5 days with no contact, leave the task overdue. Do not mark it complete.

Red = the signal that you've done your job. Management sees it as a quality callback opportunity and can re-upload the lead to the dialer for the team to work again — giving the customer another chance to pick up at a better time.
